🧬 GBM Neoantigen Discovery Pipeline

Rapid neoantigen discovery in IDH-wildtype glioblastoma through targeted sequencing

📊 Key Results

27% Patient Coverage
77% Time Reduction
89% Cost Reduction
267 Neoantigens Identified

🎯 Overview

This computational pipeline identifies clonal, tumor-expressed neoantigens in IDH-wildtype glioblastoma using a targeted 3-gene panel approach (TP53, PTEN, EGFR). The method achieves 27% patient coverage in 3 weeks compared to 16 weeks for whole-exome sequencing approaches.

✨ Key Features

⚡ Fast

3 weeks vs 16 weeks for WES. Critical in aggressive GBM with 15-month median survival.

💰 Cost-Effective

$20k vs $180k for WES. 89% cost reduction enables broader patient access.

🎯 Targeted

6 kb panel vs 30,000 kb exome. Deep sequencing (500x) for better clonality detection.

📊 Data-Driven

Population-level analysis of 320 IDH-WT GBM patients from TCGA.

🔬 Validated

Expression validated against GTEx normal brain. NetMHCpan-4.2 HLA predictions.

♻️ Reproducible

Complete pipeline with publication-ready figures. MIT licensed.
